Privacy Policy for Diva Calculator

Last updated: 2025-11-30

Diva Calculator respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Diva Calculator collects limited, non-personal information through Firebase services:

1. Firebase Crashlytics

  • Crash logs
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App version
  • Stack traces

This data is used only to diagnose and fix crashes.

2. Firebase Analytics (Basic Usage Event)

The app tracks one simple button usage event to understand how often a specific function is used.

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No advertising or profiling is performed.
  • Data is anonymous and aggregated.

Data Sharing

Crash and analytics data is processed by Google Firebase. No data is sold or shared with any other third parties.
